Collapsible Sidebar Demo

This example demonstrates a collapsible navigation sidebar suitable for documentation websites, dashboards, and admin panels. Users can hide the sidebar to maximize content space and show it again whenever needed.

The transition is smooth and provides a better reading experience, especially on smaller screens.
