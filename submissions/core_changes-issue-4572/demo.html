easemotion/misc.css includes content-visibility: auto and contain-intrinsic-size: 1px on .ease-float. core/animations.css is missing these performance optimizations, breaking the framework's claim that bundle and modular paths are equivalent.

Why it matters

content-visibility: auto defers rendering of off-screen elements, improving page load performance. Without it, every .ease-float element renders immediately even if below the fold.
