Drop Caps

Animated initial letter styles in 6 variants

Simple

The simplest drop cap style — just a larger, colored first letter. It stands out naturally without additional decoration, perfect for minimal editorial designs.

Border

Bordered drop cap with a subtle outline that frames the letter. The border adds structure and visual weight, ideal for formal publications and documentation.

Circle

Circular drop cap with gradient background and a gentle glow pulse animation. The glowing effect draws the reader's eye, making it perfect for feature articles.

Gradient

Gradient text fill that transitions between two vibrant colors. This modern effect works beautifully on landing pages and creative content where impact matters.

Outline

Outlined drop cap with transparent fill and a colored stroke. The lightweight feel of outlined text adds elegance without overwhelming the surrounding content.

Serif

Serif-styled drop cap with italic flair and a warm gold hue. This classic typographic treatment evokes traditional print and literary elegance.
