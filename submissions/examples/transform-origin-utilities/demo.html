Transform Origin Utility Classes

Control the pivot point of your rotations, scaling, and flip animations with easy-to-use utility classes.

What is transform-origin?

The transform-origin property in CSS establishes the point of origin for an element's transformations (rotations, scaling, translates, or skews). By default, this pivot point is located exactly at the center (50% 50%) of the element.

Why does the pivot point matter?

Changing the origin point alters the trajectory of animations. A rotation around the top-left corner behaves like a swinging sign, while rotation around the center behaves like a wheel. These utilities let you control this behavior instantly.
