3D axis motion

Ease Flip Axis

Axis-specific flip utilities for panels that need real perspective, opacity easing, and matching exit variants.

ease-flip-x

Horizontal axis

Starts at rotateX(90deg), fades in, and settles flat.

ease-flip-y

Vertical axis

Rotates from the Y axis with the same soft ease-out finish.

ease-flip-x-out

Exit variant

Moves out through rotateX while opacity drops.
