⚡ 8-Value Border-Radius Magic

  • 📐 Uses 8-value border-radius: 60% 40% 30% 70% / 60% 30% 70% 40%
  • 🔄 Animates between 4 different organic shapes
  • 🎨 Configurable via --ease-blob-speed and --blob-color
  • 💧 Smooth infinite morphing animation
  • 🌟 Pause on hover variant
