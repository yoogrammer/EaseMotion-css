# Compound Interest Calculator

## What does this do?
A compound interest calculator that computes final investment value with monthly contributions, configurable compounding frequency, and a visual principal vs earnings bar chart.

## How is it used?
Enter principal amount, monthly addition, annual interest rate, time period, and compounding frequency. Results show final amount, total contributions, and total interest with a comparison chart.

## Why is this useful?
Investors and savers can visualize the power of compound interest and compare how different contribution strategies and compounding frequencies affect long-term growth.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to calculate compound interest.
