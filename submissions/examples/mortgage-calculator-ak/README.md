# Mortgage Calculator

## What does this do?
A mortgage calculator that computes monthly payment, generates a yearly amortization schedule, and displays a cost breakdown pie chart comparing total principal vs total interest.

## How is it used?
Enter home price, down payment percentage, interest rate, and loan term. View monthly payment, amortization table with yearly breakdown, and a pie chart showing the principal vs interest cost split.

## Why is this useful?
This helps home buyers understand the true cost of a mortgage, compare loan scenarios, and visualize how much goes toward interest over the life of the loan.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to use the calculator.
