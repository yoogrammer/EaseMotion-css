To quickly navigate the editor, you can press ⌥ Opt + ➡️ to jump cursor word-by-word, or hold ⇧ Shift to extend the range of highlighted code.

Pressing Enter will submit the current text box. If you want to insert a hard newline instead, press Shift + Enter.

You can verify your configuration locally at ~/.config/ease/ by triggering the config update using F5.
