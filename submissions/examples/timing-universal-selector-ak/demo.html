Scoped & safe

Why scoping matters

Third-party widgets (chat embeds, analytics badges, video players) often rely on CSS animations. A universal selector override destroys those animations, leading to a degraded experience. By scoping the override to .ease-animate you only affect EaseMotion-controlled elements.

Universal selector
  • Breaks third-party embeds
  • Hard to override
  • Surprising side effects
Scoped class
  • Preserves external content
  • Easy to opt in/out
  • Predictable behavior
