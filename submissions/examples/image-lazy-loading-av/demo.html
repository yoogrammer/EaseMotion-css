Native Image loading="lazy" Refactor

This demo page simulates a very long article or documentation page. Please scroll down to the bottom.

By strictly enforcing the native loading="lazy" and decoding="async" attributes on all heavy images, the browser completely defers downloading the image asset until it is actually about to scroll into the user's viewport. This massively improves the page's initial Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) performance score.
