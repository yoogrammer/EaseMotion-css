Navbar Hover Guard Fix Fixed

Desktop (hover device): Hover over nav items — colour change applies as expected in both light and dark mode.

Mobile (touch device) in dark mode: Tap a nav item — no colour flash before navigation. The hover rule is now guarded with @media (hover: hover) and (pointer: fine), consistent with the rest of the library.

Switch your OS to dark mode and test on a touch device (or Chrome DevTools touch emulation) to verify the fix.
