:focus-visible Refactor

By migrating the framework entirely to the modern :focus-visible CSS pseudo-class, we can natively provide massive, highly visible focus rings for visually impaired keyboard users without ruining the aesthetic experience for standard mouse users!

Legacy Component (:focus)

If you click this button with your mouse, an ugly focus outline gets permanently stuck around it.

Modern Component (:focus-visible)

Clicking with your mouse does nothing! But if you hit the TAB key to focus this button natively, a beautiful purple outline instantly appears.
