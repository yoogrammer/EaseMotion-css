🌀 ease-clip-wipe

Clip-path wipe transition reveals elements from different directions

Wipe Directions (Hover to see effect)

⬅️

Wipe Left

Reveals from left

➡️

Wipe Right

Reveals from right

⬆️

Wipe Top

Reveals from top

⬇️

Wipe Bottom

Reveals from bottom

🔀

Wipe Diagonal

Reveals diagonally

Speed Variations

⚡

Fast Wipe

0.3s animation

⏱️

Normal Wipe

0.6s animation

🐢

Slow Wipe

1.2s animation

Entrance Animation (On Page Load)

✨

Entrance Wipe

Wipes in from left on load

✨

Entrance Right

Wipes in from right

Button Triggered Wipe

🔘

Click to Reveal

This card wipes in when you click the button

Custom Duration (CSS Variable)

🎛️

Custom 1s

--ease-wipe-duration: 1s

🎛️

Custom 0.4s

--ease-wipe-duration: 0.4s

Usage

<!-- Wipe directions (on hover) -->
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-left">Reveals from left</div>
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-right">Reveals from right</div>
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-top">Reveals from top</div>
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-bottom">Reveals from bottom</div>
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-diagonal">Reveals diagonally</div>

<!-- Entrance animation -->
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-entrance">Wipes in on load</div>

<!-- Custom duration -->
<div class="ease-clip-wipe-left" style="--ease-wipe-duration: 1s;">Slow wipe</div>
