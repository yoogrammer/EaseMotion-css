Our Company Journey

  1. January 2019

    Company Founded

    Three engineers quit their day jobs and rented a co-working desk to build a zero-config deployment platform they'd always wanted to use themselves.

    Founding
    🚀
  2. 💰
    September 2019

    $2.4M Seed Round

    Raised $2.4 million from Gradient Ventures and a cohort of angel investors, funding an initial team of eight and a move to a real office in Austin, TX.

    Fundraising
  3. March 2020

    Public Beta Launch

    Opened the platform to the public after six months in closed beta. Over 4,000 developers signed up within the first 48 hours, crashing the waitlist server.

    Product

    November 2021

    $18M Series A

    Led by Sequoia Capital with participation from existing angels. Used to scale the engineering team, expand data center coverage to four continents, and launch the enterprise tier.

    Fundraising
  5. June 2023

    10,000 Active Customers

    Hit 10,000 monthly active customers including Fortune 500 names across fintech, healthcare, and SaaS. Achieved SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.

  6. 🌍
