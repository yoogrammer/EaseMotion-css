# Personal Finance AI App Template
A high-tech, intelligent, and data-forward landing page template for AI-powered finance apps, robo-advisors, and automated budgeting tools.

- **Design**: Uses a "Future-Finance" palette with ai-neon highlights, wealth-emerald success accents, and dark-mode tech layouts designed to convey sophistication, security, and algorithmic precision.
- **Use Case**: AI finance assistants, investment robo-advisors, and automated wealth management platforms.
