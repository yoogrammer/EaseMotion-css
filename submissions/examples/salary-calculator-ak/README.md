# Salary Calculator

## What does this do?
An interactive salary calculator that breaks down annual salary into monthly, bi-weekly, weekly, and hourly rates, with a visual tax bracket chart showing marginal tax rates and estimated take-home pay.

## How is it used?
Enter your annual salary in the input field. The calculator instantly shows the breakdown across all pay periods and visualizes how income falls into progressive tax brackets with estimated deductions.

## Why is this useful?
This provides a comprehensive financial planning tool for employees and freelancers to understand their earnings breakdown and tax obligations at a glance.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to use the calculator.
