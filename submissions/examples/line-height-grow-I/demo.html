Understanding Typography in Web Design

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. The arrangement of type involves selecting typefaces, point sizes, line lengths, line-spacing (leading), and letter-spacing (tracking).

Line height, also known as leading, is the vertical space between lines of text. Proper line height is crucial for readability and user experience. A line height that is too tight can make text feel cramped and difficult to read, while a line height that is too loose can make text feel disconnected and disjointed.
