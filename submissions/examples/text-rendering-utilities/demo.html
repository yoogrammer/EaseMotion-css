Text Rendering Utility Classes

Hint the browser to prioritize speed, legibility, or geometric precision when rendering text via the text-rendering property. Differences are subtle and most visible with ligature-rich fonts and kerning pairs (e.g. "AV", "fi").

💡 Note: No text-rendering declarations exist anywhere in core/ or components/ today. Effect is subtle and depends on font and browser engine.
