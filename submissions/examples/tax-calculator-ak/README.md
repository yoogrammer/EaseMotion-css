# Tax Calculator

## What does this do?
An income tax calculator that applies progressive tax brackets with standard deduction, showing tax owed per bracket, total tax, effective tax rate, and take-home pay with visualization.

## How is it used?
Enter your annual income and standard deduction amount. The calculator shows taxable income, per-bracket tax breakdown with color-coded visualization, total tax owed, effective rate, and take-home percentage.

## Why is this useful?
This provides a clear understanding of how progressive taxation works, helping users estimate their tax burden and plan their finances accordingly.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to use the calculator.
