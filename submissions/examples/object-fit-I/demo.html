<!-- Contain - fits inside, may have letterbox -->
<img class="ease-object-contain" src="image.jpg" alt="Image">

<!-- On any image or video element -->
<video class="ease-object-cover"></video>

🖼️ Object-Fit Utilities

  • 📐 ease-object-cover - Fills container, maintains aspect ratio (may crop)
  • 📦 ease-object-contain - Fits inside container, maintains aspect ratio
  • 🔲 ease-object-fill - Stretches to fill container (may distort)
  • 🎯 ease-object-center - No scaling, centered in container
  • 🖼️ Works on images, videos, and iframes
  • 📱 Perfect for responsive image layouts
