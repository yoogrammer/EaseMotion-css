Small Bottom Sheet

This bottom sheet takes up 25% of the screen height.

Perfect for quick actions, confirmations, or simple messages.

Medium Bottom Sheet

This bottom sheet takes up 50% of the screen height.

Great for forms, settings, or additional options.

Large Bottom Sheet

This bottom sheet takes up 75% of the screen height.

Ideal for longer content, lists, or multi-step processes.

  • Option 1: Profile Settings
  • Option 2: Notification Preferences
  • Option 3: Privacy Controls
  • Option 4: Help & Support

Full Bottom Sheet

This bottom sheet takes up 90% of the screen height.

Almost full-screen experience while maintaining the bottom sheet gesture.
