Developer Reference

Clean & Composable Syntax

No complex boilerplate code. Standard CSS transitions bound to viewports with semantic naming.

index.html
<!-- 1. Single Viewport Reveal element -->
<div class="reveal-on-scroll reveal-fade-up">
  <h3>Standard Reveal</h3>
</div>

<!-- 2. Configurable Reveal with Delay and custom timing -->
<div class="reveal-on-scroll reveal-zoom-in reveal-ease-bounce" style="--reveal-delay: 0.2s;">
  <button>Click Me</button>
</div>

<!-- 3. Staggered Timeline Group -->
<div class="reveal-group reveal-stagger-fast">
  <div class="reveal-item reveal-fade-right">Card A</div>
  <div class="reveal-item reveal-fade-right">Card B</div>
  <div class="reveal-item reveal-fade-right">Card C</div>
</div>
