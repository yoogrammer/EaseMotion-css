prefers-reduced-motion Refactor

This architectural submission directly proposes implementing a global CSS kill-switch that definitively forces the entire framework to respect the user's OS-level WCAG accessibility settings.

Hover Me

If you have the "Reduce Motion" accessibility setting turned on in your Mac (System Settings > Accessibility > Display) or Windows environment, this card will instantly snap to its hover state instead of smoothly sliding and scaling up, safely protecting users with vestibular motion disorders.
