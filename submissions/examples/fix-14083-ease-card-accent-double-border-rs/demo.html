Fix for #14083: ease-card-accent double border

The original accent card implementation replaced the 1px left border with a 4px left border. Combined with a 1rem border-radius, this causes browsers to render an awkward diagonal corner seam that looks like a "double border" or clipping artifact.

Before (Broken)

Primary Accent

Inspect the top-left and bottom-left corners. The 4px border meets the 1px border at an angle, creating a visual rendering artifact (the "double border" effect).

Danger Accent

The seam is visible on all accent variants because they all use border-left: 4px solid.

After (Fixed)

Primary Accent

The card now maintains its perfect 1px border around all 4 sides. The 4px accent line is rendered using an absolute ::before pseudo-element, perfectly clipped by the card's overflow.

Danger Accent

No more corner seams or double border rendering artifacts!
