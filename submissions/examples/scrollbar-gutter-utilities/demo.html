scrollbar-gutter Utility Classes

scrollbar-gutter reserves space for the scrollbar track so content does not shift when a scrollbar appears or disappears. Toggle content below to see the layout shift difference.

💡 Tip: Click "Toggle content" to add/remove enough text to trigger a scrollbar. Compare how the content shifts without the utility vs stays stable with it.
.ease-scrollbar-gutter-auto (default)

Content shifts when scrollbar appears.

Notice how the text moves left as the scrollbar takes space.

.ease-scrollbar-gutter-stable

Content stays in place — scrollbar space always reserved.

No layout shift when scrollbar appears or disappears.

.ease-scrollbar-gutter-both-edges

Space reserved on both sides for symmetric centering.

Useful for centered layouts that need visual balance.

Composing with .ease-scrollbar-thin

Combine .ease-scrollbar-gutter-stable with .ease-scrollbar-thin for a polished, stable scrollable area.

Usage

<!-- Prevent layout shift on scrollable containers -->
<div class="ease-scrollbar-gutter-stable">
  ...scrollable content...
</div>

<!-- Symmetric layout with stable both-edges -->
<div class="ease-scrollbar-gutter-both-edges">
  ...centered content...
</div>

<!-- Compose with existing scrollbar utilities -->
<div class="ease-scrollbar-gutter-stable ease-scrollbar-thin">
  ...polished scrollable area...
</div>
