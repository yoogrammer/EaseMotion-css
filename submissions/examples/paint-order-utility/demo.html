Paint-Order Utility

Controls whether fill or stroke is painted first on text and SVG elements. Use paint-order-stroke to create outlined text where the stroke appears behind the fill.

Text with stroke: paint order comparison

paint-order-normal
Hello
Stroke drawn on top of fill
paint-order-stroke
Hello
Stroke drawn behind fill
paint-order-stroke-fill
Hello
Explicit: stroke then fill

With thicker stroke (3px)

paint-order-normal

Without stroke (fill only)

paint-order-fill

SVG elements

Tip: paint-order: stroke is especially useful for logo marks, hero headings, and badge text where you want a clean outline behind filled text without the stroke bleeding inward.
