text-wrap Modes

See how normal, balance, and pretty affect the same headline.

normal

Default word-wrap — no optimization

text-wrap: normal

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog near the banks of the river Thames in London, England.

balance

Equalises line lengths for headings

text-wrap: balance

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog near the banks of the river Thames in London, England.

pretty

Optimises the last line (no orphans)

text-wrap: pretty

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog near the banks of the river Thames in London, England.

Tip: Resize the container or the browser window to see how each mode re-wraps differently.

balance is best for short headings (< 5 lines). pretty prevents single-word orphans on the last line.

Requires Chrome 114+ or Firefox 121+. Falls back to normal in older browsers.
