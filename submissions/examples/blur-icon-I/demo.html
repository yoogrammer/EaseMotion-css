🔮 Blur Icon Features

  • ✨ Smooth blur transition on hover
  • 🎯 Multiple intensity levels (subtle, normal, strong)
  • ⚡ Speed variations (fast, normal, slow)
  • 🎭 Easing variations (bounce, elastic)
  • 🎨 Works on icons, buttons, and images
  • 🖱️ Focus state support for accessibility
  • 💫 Can be combined with other effects
