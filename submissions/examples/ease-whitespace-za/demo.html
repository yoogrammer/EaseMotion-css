ease-whitespace utilities

Control how whitespace and line breaks are handled in text.

white-space: normal

normal (default)
Multiple spaces collapse. Newlines are ignored. Text wraps naturally.

white-space: nowrap

nowrap
Multiple spaces collapse. No line breaks allowed.

white-space: pre

pre
Spaces preserved. Newlines respected. No wrapping.

white-space: pre-wrap

pre-wrap
Spaces preserved. Newlines respected. Text wraps.

white-space: pre-line

pre-line
Spaces collapse. Newlines respected. Text wraps naturally.

These utilities are essential for displaying code, preformatted text, addressing text, and controlling inline layout behavior.
