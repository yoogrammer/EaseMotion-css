Usage Example

<!-- Basic Tooltip --> <span class="ease-tooltip" data-tooltip="Your text here"> Hover me </span> <!-- With Position --> <span class="ease-tooltip" data-tooltip="Bottom tooltip" data-position="bottom"> Hover me </span> <!-- Wide Tooltip --> <span class="ease-tooltip" data-tooltip="Long description..." data-width="wide"> Hover me </span>

Accessibility: Tooltips are visible on both :hover and :focus-within, making them accessible via keyboard navigation. They also respect prefers-reduced-motion settings.
