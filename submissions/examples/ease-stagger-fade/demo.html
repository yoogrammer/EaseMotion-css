Usage

<!-- Basic staggered items (1s total duration) -->
<div class="ease-stagger-1">First item</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-2">Second item</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-3">Third item</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-4">Fourth item</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-5">Fifth item</div>

<!-- Fast speed (0.5s total) -->
<div class="ease-stagger-fast-1">Fast 1</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-fast-2">Fast 2</div>

<!-- Slow speed (2s total) -->
<div class="ease-stagger-slow-1">Slow 1</div>
<div class="ease-stagger-slow-2">Slow 2</div>

⚡ Staggered Fade Features

  • ✨ Smooth fade-in with slide-up effect
  • ⏱️ Incremental delays (0.1s, 0.2s, 0.3s, etc.)
  • ⚡ Speed variations (fast: 0.5s, normal: 1s, slow: 2s)
  • 🎯 Works on any element
  • 💫 Auto-fades in on page load
  • 🔄 Supports up to 6 staggered items
