All variants at a glance

.ease-bg-animate

Base class — purple → blue → cyan. 6s cycle. No extra class needed.

.ease-bg-animate.ease-bg-animate-sunset

Orange → pink → purple. Warm, energetic. Great for CTA sections.

.ease-bg-animate.ease-bg-animate-ocean

Teal → blue → indigo. Cool and calm. Ideal for tech / SaaS.

.ease-bg-animate.ease-bg-animate-forest

Green → teal → lime. Fresh and organic. Nature or health brands.

.ease-bg-animate.ease-bg-animate-dark

Dark indigo → slate → deep cyan. Dramatic. For dark-mode UIs.

.ease-bg-animate-fast  /  .ease-bg-animate-slow

Stacks on any variant. Fast = 3s cycle. Slow = 12s cycle.
