# QR Scanner Input Simulator

## What does this do?
A simulated QR scanner input with scan area animation, manual QR content entry, decoded content display card, scan history tracking, and copy-to-clipboard functionality.

## How is it used?
Click the scan area to simulate scanning a QR code, or paste QR content manually into the text area and click Decode. View decoded content in a card, copy it to clipboard, and browse scan history with timestamps.

## Why is this useful?
This provides a realistic QR scanning UI simulation for prototyping or testing without requiring actual camera access.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to use the simulator.
