Usage

<div class="ease-glass-card">
    <!-- Card content -->
</div>

<!-- Different blur intensities -->
<div class="ease-glass-card-blur-sm">Light blur</div>
<div class="ease-glass-card-blur-lg">Heavy blur</div>

<!-- Different lift amounts -->
<div class="ease-glass-card-lift-sm">Subtle lift</div>
<div class="ease-glass-card-lift-lg">Strong lift</div>

<!-- Color variants -->
<div class="glass-card-primary ease-glass-card">...</div>
<div class="glass-card-success ease-glass-card">...</div>

🔮 Glassmorphism Features

  • ✨ backdrop-filter: blur() for glass effect
  • 🎨 Semi-transparent background
  • 📈 Smooth hover lift (translateY)
  • 🎯 Blur intensity variants (sm, normal, lg)
  • 📏 Lift amount variants (sm, normal, lg)
  • 🎨 Color variants (primary, success, danger, warning, dark)
  • ⚡ Speed variations (fast, normal, slow)
  • 💎 Frosted glass border options
