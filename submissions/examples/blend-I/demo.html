Usage

<div class="ease-blend-multiply">
    <img src="image.jpg">
    <div class="overlay"></div>
</div>

<!-- Screen blend mode (lightens) -->
<div class="ease-blend-screen">
    <div class="layer"></div>
</div>

<!-- Overlay blend mode (high contrast) -->
<div class="ease-blend-overlay">
    <div class="gradient"></div>
</div>

<!-- On text -->

🎨 Mix Blend Mode Utilities

  • 🎨 ease-blend-multiply - Darkens overlapping areas
  • 💡 ease-blend-screen - Lightens overlapping areas
  • 🎭 ease-blend-overlay - High contrast blend
  • 🖼️ Perfect for creative image effects
  • 📝 Works on text, images, and layers
  • ✨ Combine with backgrounds for unique effects
