Usage

<!-- Wrap (default) -->
<div class="ease-flex-wrap">
    <div>Item 1</div>
    <div>Item 2</div>
</div>

<!-- No wrap -->
<div class="ease-flex-nowrap">
    <div>Item 1</div>
    <div>Item 2</div>
</div>

<!-- Wrap reverse -->
<div class="ease-flex-wrap-reverse">
    <div>Item 1</div>
    <div>Item 2</div>
</div>

📐 Flex Wrap Utilities

  • 📦 ease-flex-wrap - Items wrap to next line
  • 📏 ease-flex-nowrap - Items stay on one line
  • 🔄 ease-flex-wrap-reverse - Items wrap in reverse order
  • 🎯 Perfect for responsive layouts
  • 📱 Essential for card grids, button groups, tag clouds
