⚡ Squash and Stretch Physics

  • 📐 Stretches horizontally: scaleX(1.25)
  • 📏 Squashes vertically: scaleY(0.75)
  • 🔄 Then reverses the stretch
  • 💪 Returns to original shape
  • 🎯 Triggered on :active (click/tap)
