steps() Timing

Frame-by-frame animation — like a flipbook or sprite sheet.

steps(4) — 4 distinct frames

steps(8) — 8 distinct frames

steps(4, jump-none)

steps() divides the animation into equal discrete jumps — useful for typewriters, loading dots, and sprite animations. Compare steps(4) (4 jumps) vs steps(8) (8 jumps) vs jump-none (smooth start/end).
