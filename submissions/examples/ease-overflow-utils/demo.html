📦 ease-overflow utilities

Control how content overflows its container

Overflow Hidden

ease-overflow-hidden

This content is very long and would normally overflow its container. But with overflow hidden, the excess content is clipped and not visible.

Clipped content is hidden

Overflow Auto

ease-overflow-auto

This content is very long and overflows. With overflow auto, scrollbars appear only when needed. Try scrolling to see the hidden content.

Scrollbar appears when needed

Overflow Scroll

ease-overflow-scroll

This content is very long. With overflow scroll, scrollbars always appear (even if content fits), allowing you to scroll through the content.

Scrollbars always visible

Axis-Specific Overflow

ease-overflow-x-hidden

This content is wide and would overflow horizontally, but overflow-x-hidden clips only the horizontal overflow.

Horizontal overflow hidden

ease-overflow-y-hidden

This content is tall. Vertical overflow is hidden, but horizontal may still overflow.

Vertical overflow hidden

⚡ Overflow Utilities

  • ease-overflow-hidden - Clips overflowing content
  • ease-overflow-auto - Scrollbars appear when needed
  • ease-overflow-scroll - Scrollbars always visible
  • ease-overflow-x-hidden - Hides horizontal overflow only
  • ease-overflow-y-hidden - Hides vertical overflow only
