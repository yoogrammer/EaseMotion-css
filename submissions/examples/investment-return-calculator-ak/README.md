# Investment Return Calculator

## What does this do?
A compound investment calculator that models growth with principal, monthly contributions, annual return rate, investment period, and configurable compounding frequency, with a visual growth chart.

## How is it used?
Enter your principal, monthly contribution, expected annual return, investment period in years, and compounding frequency. The calculator shows total value, contributions, earnings, and a stacked bar chart of growth.

## Why is this useful?
This helps investors visualize the power of compound interest and understand how different contribution amounts and return rates affect long-term investment growth.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser to use the calculator.
