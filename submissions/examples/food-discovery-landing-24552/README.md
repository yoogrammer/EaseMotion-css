# Food Discovery App Template
A vibrant, appetizing, and user-centric landing page template for restaurant discovery apps, food-tracking platforms, and local culinary guides.

- **Design**: Uses a "Flavor-Fresh" palette with flavor-orange primary branding, fresh-mint success accents, and warm-creamy backgrounds designed to stimulate appetite and promote effortless exploration.
- **Use Case**: Restaurant recommendation apps, culinary discovery platforms, and hyper-local food guides.
