TYPOGRAPHIC LIQUID EFFECT

Liquid Morphing Text

Organic Fluid Transitions in Pure CSS

A display banner where text melts into fluid blobs and merges seamlessly into the letters of the next word. Powered entirely by SVG filters and staggered CSS opacity animations.

Creative Performant Animations

Technical Specifications

  • feGaussianBlur & feColorMatrix — The blur expands character outlines, and the matrix contrast multiplication (alpha x 20, subtract 9) sharpens the blurred boundaries into fluid blobs.
  • Staggered 12s Animation Cycle — Cycles 3 words sequentially with 4s intervals by offsetting `animation-delay` (0s, 4s, and 8s) on a single keyframe rule.
  • Dynamic Letter Spacing — Compresses letters to -0.15em on entry to form a centralized liquid blob, then expands to 0.18em on exit as the characters dissolve.
  • prefers-reduced-motion Overrides — Disables SVG contrast filters, blurs, and translations, fading the words in and out statically to prevent motion sickness.
