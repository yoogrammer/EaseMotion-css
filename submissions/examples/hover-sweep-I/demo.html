Usage

<!-- Sweep from left to right -->
<button class="ease-hover-sweep-left">Sweep Left →</button>

<!-- Sweep from right to left -->
<button class="ease-hover-sweep-right">← Sweep Right</button>

<!-- Sweep from top to bottom -->
<button class="ease-hover-sweep-top">Sweep Top ↓</button>

<!-- Sweep from bottom to top -->
<button class="ease-hover-sweep-bottom">↑ Sweep Bottom</button>

<!-- Custom color -->
<div class="ease-hover-sweep-left" style="--sweep-color: #ff6b6b;">
    Custom Color Sweep
</div>

<!-- Different speeds -->
<div class="ease-hover-sweep-left-fast">Fast sweep</div>
<div class="ease-hover-sweep-left-slow">Slow sweep</div>

🧹 Hover Sweep Features

  • 📐 Background sweeps in from one side on hover
  • 🎯 4 directions (left, right, top, bottom)
  • 🎨 Customizable color via --sweep-color variable
  • ⚡ Speed variations (fast, normal, slow)
  • 🌈 Supports gradient sweeps
  • 💫 Uses background-size: 200% + background-position transition
