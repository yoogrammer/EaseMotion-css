Motion Personas + Intent-Based Motion

Choose the motion personality and the intent, then let CSS produce the right animation by combining both.

Personas

Personas define the motion feel: premium, playful, minimal, energetic, or cinematic.

Premium

Elegant, smooth, refined motion for polished interfaces.

Playful

Bouncy, dynamic, friendly motion for lively dashboards.

Minimal

Fast, subtle, low-motion behavior for simple UIs.

Energetic

Fast-paced, attention-grabbing motion for high-energy apps.

Cinematic

Slow, dramatic, storytelling-focused motion.

Intents

Intents describe why the motion happens: introduce, highlight, confirm, notify, celebrate, or warn.

Introduce

Present new content gently with a smooth reveal.

Highlight

Draw attention with a subtle emphasis motion.

Confirm

Reinforce successful actions with a polished response.

Notify

Inform users of updates with calm and reliable motion.

Celebrate

Use a stronger motion for achievements and milestones.

Warn

Bring attention to cautionary or critical states.

Combined examples

Combine a persona with an intent for semantic animation that matches both personality and purpose.
