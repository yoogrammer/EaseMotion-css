:focus-within Refactor

Please click anywhere inside this window and then press the Tab key multiple times to navigate purely via keyboard.

Notice how when you Tab onto the "Dropdown Menu" button, and then Tab again to enter the internal links, the dropdown stays open! This is because the parent wrapper uses the modern :focus-within pseudo-class to natively detect that a child element has focus, without requiring any complex JavaScript.
