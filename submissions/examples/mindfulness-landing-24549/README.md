# Mindfulness Meditation App Template
A serene, balanced, and aesthetically pleasing landing page template for meditation apps, mindfulness platforms, and wellness-focused digital products.

- **Design**: Uses a "Zen-Harmony" palette with calm-sand backgrounds, zen-sage branding elements, and rounded card designs to evoke a sense of quietude, accessibility, and mental balance.
- **Use Case**: Meditation apps, mental health platforms, and digital wellness products.
