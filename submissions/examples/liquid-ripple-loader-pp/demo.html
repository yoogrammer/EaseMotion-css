Liquid Ripple Loader

An organic, satisfying page loader spinner utilizing pure CSS keyframe loops and inline SVG gooey filters. Synchronizes droplet gravity acceleration, base pool impact, and ripples.

How it works

  • SVG Gooey Shader: Uses a `feGaussianBlur` filter with `stdDeviation="9"`, followed by a `feColorMatrix` to compress the alpha channel. This creates a sharp liquid-like surface tension overlay when shapes overlap.
  • Physics Simulation: Droplet accelerates downwards using a cubic bezier curve, stretching to `scale(0.7, 1.4)` during flight, and squashing horizontally to `scale(1.6, 0.4)` on base impact.
  • Reactive base pool: The pool squashes and wobbles vertically exactly at `30%` of the cycle, matching the droplet's splash down timeline.
  • Wave Ripples: Dynamic concentric rings scale up and thin out from the splash point immediately upon droplet impact, fully integrated inside the gooey filter wrapper.
  • Accessibility: Automatically switches to a simple rotating fallback spinner for users with motion sensitivities under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
