Large headings: why thickness matters

At large sizes, the default underline (1px) looks too thin. A thicker underline creates better visual balance.

Default
Thicker (3px)
Bold (4px)
Tip: Combine text-decoration-thickness with text-underline-offset for precise link underline styling. A 2-3px underline with 3-4px offset is a common pattern for clean, readable links.
