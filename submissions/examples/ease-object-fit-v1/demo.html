Object Fit & Position

Sleek, atomic layout utilities for controlling how images and videos resize and align inside their aspect-ratio containers.

Object Fit (ease-object-*)

.ease-object-cover

Cover

Resizes the image to fill the container while preserving aspect ratio. Parts of the image may be clipped.

.ease-object-contain

Contain

Resizes the image to fit entirely inside the container while preserving aspect ratio. Letterboxing will occur.

.ease-object-fill

Fill

Stretches or squishes the image to completely fill the container, disregarding the native aspect ratio.

.ease-object-none

None

Keeps the image at its original, native dimensions. The container acts as a window clipper.

.ease-object-scale-down

Scale Down

Acts as either "None" or "Contain", whichever results in a smaller scale size for the media content.

Object Position (ease-object-[position])

.ease-object-center

Center

Centers the content inside the parent boundaries. (Default browser behavior)

.ease-object-top

Top

Anchors the content to the top edge of the layout boundaries.

.ease-object-bottom

Bottom

Anchors the content to the bottom edge of the layout boundaries.

.ease-object-left

Left

Anchors the content to the left edge of the layout boundaries.

.ease-object-right

Right

Anchors the content to the right edge of the layout boundaries.

Developer Guide

Combine fit and position classes to perfectly frame your media inside fixed-height or dynamic aspect-ratio container blocks: 

<div class="media-wrapper ease-relative ease-overflow-hidden">
  <img 
    src="hero.jpg" 
    class="ease-w-full ease-h-full ease-object-cover ease-object-top" 
  />
</div>
