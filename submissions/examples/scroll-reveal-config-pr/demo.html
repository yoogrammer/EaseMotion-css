Scroll Reveal Customizer

Scroll down to see the customized scroll animations in action. Different sections use different thresholds, delays, and repeat configurations.

↓
Delay Option

Delayed Trigger (600ms)

This card has data-reveal-delay="600". When it enters the viewport, it waits for 600 milliseconds before firing the fade-in animation.

Threshold Option

High Threshold (60% Visible)

This card has data-reveal-threshold="0.6". The slide-up reveal will not fire until 60% of the card is scrolled into the viewport.

Repeat Option

Re-triggerable (once="false")

This card has data-reveal-once="false". If you scroll past it or scroll up away from it, the animation resets. It will animate again every single time you scroll back to it.
