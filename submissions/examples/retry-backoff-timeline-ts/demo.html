Webhook delivery

Retry backoff timeline

A CSS-only sequence for explaining failed attempts, wait windows, and recovery after exponential backoff.

  1. Attempt 1 failed

    Server returned 503. Retry scheduled after 500ms.

    500ms
  2. Attempt 2 failed

    Connection opened, response timed out.

    1s
  3. Backoff window

    Request paused to reduce pressure on the endpoint.

    2s
  4. Attempt 3 delivered

    Webhook acknowledged and removed from the queue.

    200 OK
