Mobile Grid Graceful Degradation Demo

Resize your browser between 480px and 640px (large phones / small tablets).

Normally in the core framework, a 4-column grid would try to squash into 4 columns here (causing overflow), and then suddenly jump to 1 column below 480px.

With this patch, the 4-column grid gracefully degrades to exactly 2 columns between 480px and 640px, and then 1 column for phones!
