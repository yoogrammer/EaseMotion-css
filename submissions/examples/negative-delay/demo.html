Negative animation-delay

Start animations partway through their cycle — useful for staggered, in-sync effects.

Staggered Wave (negative delay)

Each dot uses animation-delay: calc(var(--i) * -0.15s). Negative delay means they start at different points in the same cycle — creating a seamless wave.

Compare: positive vs negative delay

Positive (0.3s)
Negative (-0.3s)

Positive delay waits before starting. Negative delay jumps 0.3s into the animation — no wait, different visual phase.
