# Podcast App Template
A modern, auditory-focused, and content-rich landing page template for podcast players, audio storytelling platforms, and digital content distribution services.

- **Design**: Uses an "Audio-Vibrant" palette with purple-to-coral gradients, dark-mode content backgrounds, and sleek, minimalist cards designed to prioritize content exploration and listener immersion.
- **Use Case**: Podcast listening apps, audio drama platforms, and digital content hubs.
