Usage

<!-- Basic testimonial -->
<div class="ease-testimonial">
    <div class="testimonial-avatar">
        <img src="avatar.jpg" alt="Avatar">
    </div>
    <div class="testimonial-content">
        <p class="testimonial-quote">Quote text here</p>
        <h4 class="testimonial-author">Author Name</h4>
        <p class="testimonial-title">Author Title</p>
    </div>
</div>

<!-- With stars -->
<div class="ease-testimonial">
    <div class="testimonial-avatar">...</div>
    <div class="testimonial-content">
        <div class="testimonial-stars">★★★★★</div>
        <p class="testimonial-quote">Quote</p>
        <h4 class="testimonial-author">Author</h4>
    </div>
</div>

⚡ Testimonial Card Features

  • 💬 Decorative quote marks using ::before pseudo-element
  • 🖼️ Avatar image slot with rounded design
  • 📝 Quote text, author name, and title slots
  • 🎨 Multiple variants (primary, dark, glass, centered)
  • ⭐ Optional rating stars support
  • 📱 Fully responsive design
