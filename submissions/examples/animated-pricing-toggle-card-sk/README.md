# Animated Pricing Toggle Card

**What does this do?**  
Two pricing cards (Basic / Pro) displayed side by side. On hover, the card lifts upward and the CTA button transitions to a glowing gradient. The Pro card is marked with a "Popular" badge and has a subtly different background treatment.

**How is it used?**  
Open `demo.html` in a browser. The cards respond to hover individually. On narrow screens (≤540px) they stack vertically. To adjust pricing or features, edit the HTML directly; colors and spacing are controlled through CSS variables and selectors.

**Why is it useful?**  
Pricing tables are a staple of SaaS and product pages. Subtle hover feedback — card lift plus a glowing CTA — provides tactile visual feedback that helps users compare plans and encourages interaction. The pattern is pure CSS, making it trivially portable across frameworks and build pipelines.
