# Enterprise CRM Intelligence Command Center

## What does this do?

The Enterprise CRM Intelligence Command Center is a boardroom-grade customer analytics and pipeline management workspace. It features interactive navigation viewpoints, lead metric summary scorecards, a sales pipeline Kanban board, customer 360 profile views, interaction timelines, active campaign CTR metrics, service escalations lists, quota trackers, and AI next-best-action recommenders.

## How is it used?

Open `demo.html` directly in any modern browser:

- **Switch Views**: Use the sidebar to pivot between Overview, Kanban Pipeline, and Customer Registry panels.
- **Select Profiles**: Click cards in the Kanban pipeline to instantly load that client's details into the Customer 360 profile view and populate their specific history timeline.
- **Interactive Timelines**: Type notes inside the activity field in the Customer 360 panel and press Enter to instantly append events to the history stream.
- **Forecast Computations**: Click "Refresh Forecast" to trigger the AI forecaster. Watch the laser line scan down the chart as projected ARR bars dynamically re-scale.
- **Quota Calculators**: Shift the Quota slider to update venture MRR targets, recalculate remaining deficits, and shift the progress percentages.
- **AI Actions**: Click "Apply" on recommendations cards to trigger automated communications and view operational logs updates.

## Why is it useful?

Managing enterprise sales cycles, tracing client interaction timelines, and predicting quarterly MRR quotas requires real-time workspace integrations to prevent information gaps.

This command center solves sales intelligence workflows by:

1. Displaying client details and chronological events side-by-side using unified flex grids.
2. Integrating a responsive quota tracker that updates progress bar metrics and targets dynamically.
3. Incorporating interactive viewpoint controls to pivot information maps from sales boards to executive forecast charts.

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (forecast bar scale transitions, scanning sweeps, pulsing beacons, progress bar fills, layout grids)
- JavaScript (in-memory profiles selector, timeline appenders, target MRR calculators, AI recommendations click resolvers)
