System Font Stack Refactor

We are intentionally forcing the primary web font ('Inter') to fail loading in this demo. Watch closely how the legacy stack completely breaks down, while the modern stack gracefully falls back to your operating system's beautiful, highly-optimized native font.

❌ Legacy Fallback

Broken Typography

Because the custom 'Inter' font failed to load, I violently fell back to the most basic 'sans-serif'. Depending on your specific browser, I might look like Times New Roman or incredibly ugly Arial. My physical letter spacing is completely broken, causing massive Cumulative Layout Shifts (CLS).

✅ Modern System Fallback

Native OS Typography

Because 'Inter' failed to load, I intelligently and seamlessly fell back to your device's physical native system font (like San Francisco on Mac/iOS, or Segoe UI on Windows). I look perfectly crisp, native, and identical to the rest of your operating system.
