🐛 Navbar Dark Mode Fix

Adds missing dark mode styles to the glass navbar component.

Navbar Demo

Toggle dark mode to see how the navbar adapts. Without the fix, the navbar would remain white and its text would become unreadable against a dark background.

How to test:
1. By default, the navbar looks normal in light mode (white glass, dark text).
2. Click the "Switch to Dark Mode" button.
3. The page background will darken, and the navbar will invert to a dark glass tint with light text.
4. Without the fix: The navbar retains its hardcoded white glass and dark text, making the text very hard to read against a dark body background.
