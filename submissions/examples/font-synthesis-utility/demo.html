Font-Synthesis Utility

Controls whether the browser may synthesize missing font faces (bold, italic, small-caps).

font-synthesis-weight (synthesize bold only)

<div class="sample font-synthesis-weight"><b>bold</b> text</div>
Bold and italic text (only bold is synthesized)

font-synthesis-style (synthesize italic only)

<div class="sample font-synthesis-style"><i>italic</i> text</div>
Bold and italic text (only italic is synthesized)

font-synthesis-weight-style (synthesize both — default)

<div class="sample font-synthesis-weight-style"><b>bold</b> <i>italic</i></div>
Bold and italic text (both are synthesized)

font-synthesis-none (no synthesis)

<div class="sample font-synthesis-none"><b>bold</b> text</div>
Bold and italic text (neither is synthesized — falls back to regular weight)
