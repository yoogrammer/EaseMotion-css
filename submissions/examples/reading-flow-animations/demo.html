Reading Flow Animations

Enhance long-form copy with subtle reveal, highlight, and progress utilities that keep content readable and guide users naturally through articles, docs, and tutorials.

Introduction

Reading Flow Animations are designed to improve long-form content without overpowering it. They use soft motion to reveal text, highlight key notes, and provide a lightweight progress bar experience.

Usage

Apply .ease-reading-reveal to text blocks or sections for a gentle fade-and-rise entrance. Use .ease-reading-highlight for notes and warnings that should stay readable but stand out subtly.

Note: This content is meant for article callouts, warnings, and tutorial tips where readability is the priority.

Blog Progress

Use .ease-reading-progress to display a subtle progress indicator for long-form reading sections.

Example article layout

Long-form text benefits from a subtle entrance animation that preserves reading flow. Soft motion helps guide the eye without distracting from the content itself.

  • Use reveal on headings, paragraphs, and section cards.
  • Apply highlight only to key information or callouts.
  • Progress indicators should remain minimal and unobtrusive.
